Valhalla's Banquet Wagon

Meet the Founder

Valhalla's Banquet Wagon was founded by Jacob Lausier, a young entrepreneur with over a decade of experience in the service industry. From his early days as a line cook to managing three restaurants within a bustling hotel, Jacob has brought his passion, leadership, and love for Norse mythology to the streets with a culinary vision worthy of the gods.

Catering & Food Truck Schedule

We cater events of all sizes — from intimate gatherings to large-scale feasts! Let Valhalla's Banquet Wagon bring the taste of the gods to your next celebration. We post our weekly location and hours on our Instagram, so follow us to find out where the wagon will land next!
